LINKEDU PRIVACY POLICY

The following are the terms and conditions of accessing and using LinkedU.com and LinkedU windows store apps and LinkedU android apps. The website LinkedU.com as well as LinkedU windows store apps (hereinafter 'LinkedU') , developed and operated by SMS Country Networks Private Limited is offered to the user/s (hereinafter 'visitor/s') upon the terms and conditions that follow. LinkedU.com is an internet and mobile service that allows users to create personal web profiles and communicate and share content with friends and other users. To help our members identify and interact with each other, we request and display some personal information to other members and visitors. LinkedU.com members can change their profile information at any time and can control how other members and the service communicates with them. LinkedU.com cares deeply about online privacy. If you have any questions concerning this privacy policy, please email us at contactus[at]LinkedU[dot]com PROCESS OF INFORMATION COLLECTION AND USE BY LINKEDU.COM LinkedU.com collects user submitted information such as name, email address, and age to authenticate users and to send notifications to those users relating to the LinkedU.com service. LinkedU.com also collects other profile data including but not limited to: personal interests, gender, age, education and occupation in order to assist users in connecting with each other. Further, in order to facilitate commerce, we collect credit card information, member's address, or bank information only from those who choose to join LinkedU.com LinkedU.com also logs non-personally-identifiable information including IP address, profile information, aggregate user data, and browser type, from users and visitors to the site This data is used to manage the website, track usage and improve the website services. This non-personally-identifiable information may be shared with third-parties to provide more relevant services and advertisements to members. User IP addresses are recorded for security and monitoring purposes. User Profile information including members' pictures and user names are displayed to people in order to facilitate user interaction in the LinkedU.com social networking community. Email addresses are used for the purposes of inviting new friends to join LinkedU.com, to add users to members' friends' networks, and to send notifications related to the service. With the exception of inviting friends, adding friends, and notifications, a user's email address is not shared or displayed to people within a user's personal network. Users within a personal network communicate on LinkedU.com with each other through the LinkedU.com service, without disclosing their email addresses. Users' full names are never directly revealed to other members. To facilitate searching and finding friends and acquaintances on the service, LinkedU.com allows users to search for other members using first and last name, email address, interests and schools where users may have attended. We may also use a user's email address to send updates, a newsletter or news regarding the service. Users may choose not to receive email of this type by changing their "notification" setting to "Do not send me notification emails" in the user "Account Settings". From time to time, LinkedU.com or a partner may advertise on LinkedU.com. Users may be asked to provide personal information including name, email address or home address in order to participate or receive a response from LinkedU.com or one of its partners. We may transfer personal information to certain ad partners that you have explicitly requested to receive information from or expressed an interest in. USE OF COOKIES LinkedU.com uses cookies to store visitors' preferences and to record session information, for purposes including ensuring that visitors are not repeatedly offered the same advertisements and to customize newsletter, advertising, and Web page content based on browser type and user profile information. We do not link the information we store in cookies to any personally identifiable information you submit while on our site. You may be able to configure your browser to accept or reject all or some cookies, or notify you when a cookie is set -- each browser is different, so check the "Help" menu of your browser to learn how to change your cookie preferences -- however, you must enable cookies from LinkedU.com in order to use most functions on the site Please note that LinkedU.com allows 3rd party advertisers that are presenting advertisements on some of our pages to set and access their cookies on your computer. Advertiser's use of cookies is subject to their own privacy policies, not the LinkedU.com Privacy Policy. INVITATIONS AND COMMUNICATION TO NON-MEMBERS LinkedU.com members can invite friends to join the service by sending invitation emails via our automated invitation system. LinkedU.com stores the email addresses that members provide so that the respondents may be added to the friend's list of the member sending the invitations, and also to send reminders of the invitations. LinkedU.com does not sell these email addresses or use them to send any other communication besides invitations. Recipients of invitations from LinkedU.com may contact LinkedU.com to request the removal of their information from our database.
